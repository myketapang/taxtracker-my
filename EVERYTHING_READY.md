## 📚 TAX KNOWLEDGE HIGHLIGHTS

Covered in the knowledge base:

**Relief Categories**:
- Personal (self, spouse, children, parents)
- Medical (checkups, dental, optical)
- Health (insurance, life insurance)
- Education (tuition, SSPN/PTPTN)
- Housing (loan interest - first 10 years only)
- Lifestyle (books, internet, sports, gym)

**Key Numbers**:
- Self Relief: RM 9,000
- Spouse: RM 9,000
- Children: RM 3,000 each (max 4)
- Medical: RM 5,000
- Education: RM 6,000
- Lifestyle: RM 1,000
- Total potential: RM 60,000+

**Important Rules**:
- Keep receipts for 7 years
- File by 30 June each year
- Housing interest only for first 10 years
- Cannot claim same item twice
- Claim within relief limits
